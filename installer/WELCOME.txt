Welcome to SoundShare!

Stream your PC audio to any phone, tablet, or computer on your Wi-Fi.
Listeners open a link in their browser — no app install needed.

WHAT GETS INSTALLED
-------------------
- SoundShare application
- VB-Audio Virtual Cable (virtual speaker driver)
- Desktop shortcut
- Windows Firewall rule (optional, port 8765)

AFTER INSTALL
-------------
1. Launch SoundShare from your Desktop
2. Your browser opens automatically
3. Share the "Network" URL with listeners on the same Wi-Fi
4. Play audio on your PC — they hear it live on their devices

No physical speakers required. No extra configuration.
