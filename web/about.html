What is SoundShare?

SoundShare streams your PC system audio to phones, tablets, and other devices on your local network. Listeners open a link in their browser — no app install required. Multiple devices can listen at once with low-latency WebRTC audio.

The Windows package includes a virtual audio driver so SoundShare works even without physical speakers connected. Everything is configured automatically on install.
